# SHIRYEN: SAIL AND STEAM
## Limited Archetypes: Enemy colors

---

Continuing on with Sail and Steam's limited archetypes, in this article we'll discover the five enemy-color strategies. If you missed the allied-color archetypes, you can find them [here](https://magictheegg.github.io/articles/shiryen-sail-and-steam/limited-archetypes-part-1).

---

## WHITE/BLACK: ARTIFACTS

In Orzhov, your charge will be to collect the scraps that every other architect has left behind. As they say, one person's trash is another's treasure, and this archetype is full of value in the form of Thopters, Constructs, and Vehicles ready to click, clack, and whir their way to the top.

![Greaseworks Mutt](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/185.png)![Prosthetic Thug](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/211.png)![Lymph Mechanist](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/165.png)

<br>
## BLUE/RED: INNOVATE

Izzet has always been the color pair of scientific insanity, and its role in SAS is no different. Your blue and red creatures will have plenty of big ideas, and your simple task will be to find ways to get them tapped and unlock their full potential.

![Aspiring Weldwright](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/198.png)![Decatech Advancements](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/236.png)![Bonny Lass](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/171.png)

<br>
## BLACK/GREEN: TOKENS

In Golgari, the creatures of Sail and Steam care mostly about the little things. You'll be rewarded for every piece of cardboard you put into play, so grab your binoculars and get ready to appreciate the small world of plants, cogs, incursions, and so much more.

![Port Pearl Curator](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/215.png)![Sow the Sea's Seed](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/240.png)![Greensea Savage](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/168.png)

<br>
## RED/WHITE: VEHICLES

In perhaps the least surprising twist in Custom Magic history, Boros in Sail and Steam is all about its ships. Whether by sea or by sky, grab a handy crew of helmsmen and bosuns and prepare to sail your all-powerful armada to new horizons!

![Stalker's Sloop](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/234.png)![Sovereign Galleon](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/192.png)![First Fleet Watchtower](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/174.png)

<br>
## GREEN/BLUE: SEA MONSTERS

Lurking in the depths of the Three Seas, you'll find Simic and its menagerie of sea beasts. Whether you're casting Leviathans, Octopi, and Serpents, or chasing Krakens sighted from the bestiary, you can rest easy knowing you'll have the full might of the waters just behind you.

![Crunch](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/251.png)![Benthic Soothsayer](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/200.png)![Krill Seekers](https://magictheegg.github.io/sets/SAS-files/img/169.png)
